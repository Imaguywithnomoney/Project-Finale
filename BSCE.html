Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering
Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering WHAT IS IT? The Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering (BSCE) is a professional degree program with a focus on the construction, design, and maintenance of infrastructure, including but not limited to buildings, bridges, roads, dams, and water supply systems. The program prepares graduates to become licensed civil engineers and hence help develop safe, sustainable, and efficient buildings and communities. Students acquire a solid mathematics, physics, and engineering science basis, as well as practical experience gained through fieldwork, laboratory experiments, and projects.
